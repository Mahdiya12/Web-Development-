Here are the places!

Taj Mahal in India

The Taj Mahal is a white marble monument built by Shah Jahan for his wife. Its beauty and romantic story attract millions of visitors every year. It is admired for its symmetry, carvings, and peaceful gardens.

Leaning Tower of Pisa in Italy

The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a famous bell tower known for its unintended tilt. Built over 800 years ago, it attracts tourists with its unique slant and beautiful Romanesque design. Visitors often take fun photos “holding up” the tower.

Mount Fuji in Japan

Mount Fuji is a beautiful and iconic volcano known for its perfect cone shape. It is a popular spot for hiking, sightseeing, and photography, especially during sunrise. Snow covers its peak in colder months, adding to its charm.

Opera House in Australia

The Sydney Opera House is a world-famous building known for its unique sail-like design. It hosts concerts, theatre shows, and events, attracting millions of tourists each year. Its location by the water makes it a perfect photo spot.

Eiffel Tower in France

The Eiffel Tower is a famous iron landmark known for its impressive height and unique design. It offers stunning views of the city from its observation decks and attracts millions of visitors every year. It’s a symbol of romance and Paris itself.
